# Incident Log Template  
**XXXXXX**  
A structured template for capturing all actions, decisions, and observations during a cyber incident.

---

## Purpose
To maintain an authoritative chronological record of all events, decisions, and actions taken during incident response.  
This log ensures accurate reconstruction, accountability, and compliance with forensic and regulatory expectations.

---

## Usage Instructions
- Record **every significant action or observation**.  
- Use **24-hour time format**.  
- Do **not** delete or modify previous entries — append only.  
- Each entry must include the **author’s name**.

---
