# Incident Response — Evidence Templates (GRC Portfolio)

This document contains reusable evidence templates demonstrating how incident response activities, reviews, and targeted risk assessments are captured and documented in a PCI DSS 4.0 aligned environment.

---

## 1. Risk Analysis Evidence Template (per PCI DSS 12.3.1)

**Document Title:** Targeted Risk Analysis – IR Training Frequency  
**Owner:** Security / GRC Team  
**Review Cycle:** Annual or following major incident  

### Sections:
1. **Scope**
   - Roles included (on-call engineers, IR leads, escalation contacts)
   - Processes evaluated (triage, containment, communication)

2. **Threat Landscape**
   - Recent phishing, ransomware, supply chain trends
   - Industry-reported IR gaps (communication delays, missed escalations)

3. **Internal Trends**
   - Post-incident review findings
   - Training gaps observed
   - Confusion during escalation

4. **Risk Scoring**
   - Likelihood (High/Medium/Low)
   - Impact (High/Medium/Low)
   - Justification text

5. **Decision**
   - “Based on the above evaluation, IR training frequency is set to:  
     **Semi-annually**.”

6. **Approval**
   - Name  
   - Title  
   - Signature or ticket link  
   - Date

## 3. Meeting Notes Template (to link TRA → Schedule)

**Meeting Title:** IR Risk Review & Training Frequency Decision  
**Attendees:** IR Lead, Security Manager, GRC Analyst  
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD  

**Agenda:**
- Review TRA results  
- Evaluate internal IR performance  
- Set training frequency  

**Decision:**  
_Example: “Based on observed escalation delays and increased ransomware risk, frequency set to **every 6 months**.”_

**Actions:**
- Update IR Plan  
- Publish training schedule  
- Notify managers
