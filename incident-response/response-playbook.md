# Cyber Incident Response Playbook  
**XXXXXX**

A structured playbook describing step-by-step actions during the incident lifecycle.

---

# 1. Preparation
- Maintain updated asset inventory  
- Train staff on incident reporting  
- Conduct regular phishing tests  
- Ensure log retention and monitoring  
- Test backups and DR plans  

---

# 2. Identification
- Detect suspicious events  
- Validate indicators of compromise  
- Collect initial logs  
- Classify incident  
- Notify IMT  

---

# 3. Containment

### Short-Term
- Isolate affected devices  
- Disable compromised accounts  
- Block malicious IPs or domains  

### Long-Term
- Apply patches  
- Reset credentials  
- Introduce additional monitoring  

---

# 4. Eradication
- Remove malware  
- Delete malicious accounts  
- Reset system configurations  
- Validate system integrity  

---

# 5. Recovery
- Restore from clean backups  
- Re-enable services gradually  
- Monitor for re-infection  
- Validate system stability  

---

# 6. Lessons Learned
- Conduct post-incident review  
- Update policies and procedures  
- Address root causes  
- Update training programs  

---
