# Annual TRA Review Policy

### Objective
Define the governance structure ensuring Targeted Risk Analyses (TRAs) are reviewed, validated, and updated annually.

---

## 1. Policy Requirements

- All TRAs must be reviewed every 12 months  
- Reviewer must confirm:
  - Assets remain accurate  
  - Threats are still relevant  
  - Likelihood/impact scoring is valid  
  - Justification for control frequency is still correct  
- Any changes in environment or tooling require TRA update  

---

## 2. Review Documentation

Each review must include:
- Date of review  
- Reviewer name  
- Decision (“Valid”, “Updated”, or “Needs Revision”)  
- Summary of changes (if any)  
- Updated TRA version control  

---

## 3. Evidence Requirements

- TRA document  
- TRA inventory spreadsheet  
- Approval signature (digital or written)  
- Audit trail  

---

## 4. Compliance Alignment
Supports:
- PCI DSS 12.3.1  
- Internal risk management framework
