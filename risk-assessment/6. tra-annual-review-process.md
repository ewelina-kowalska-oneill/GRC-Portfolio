# Annual Review Process for Targeted Risk Analyses (TRA)

## Objective
Ensure all Targeted Risk Analyses remain valid, accurate, and aligned with the organisationâ€™s threat landscape and controls.

---

## Process Requirements

### **1. Annual Review**
Each TRA must be reviewed at least every 12 months.

### **2. Review Criteria**
Each TRA must include:
- Assets in scope  
- Relevant threats  
- Likelihood and impact analysis  
- Justification of frequency/control  
- Decision and approval  

### **3. Update Requirements**
If environment, processes, or threats change:
- TRA must be updated  
- Version control maintained  
- New reviewer sign-off required  

### **4. Evidence Requirements**
- Dated TRA document  
- Reviewer name  
- Statement confirming validity or required updates  
- TRA inventory mapping  

---

## Outcome
Annual review ensures:
- Continuous alignment with regulatory frameworks  
- Readiness for external audits  
- Accurate and risk-based control frequency decisions
