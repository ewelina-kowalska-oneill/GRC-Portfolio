# Risk Register Template  
**XXXXX**

A central repository for recording, tracking, and managing organisational risks.

---

## Usage
This template follows common GRC, ISO 27005, CRISC, and NIST RMF conventions.

Each risk entry should reflect:

- clear description  
- causes and consequences  
- inherent and residual scoring  
- controls  
- risk owner  
- treatment plan

## Definitions

### **Likelihood**
- Low  
- Medium  
- High  

### **Impact**
- Minimal  
- Moderate  
- Significant  
- Critical  

### **Rating (Risk Level)**
Risk level can be derived using:

```text
Risk Rating = Likelihood Ã— Impact
```

## Notes
- Risk register must be reviewed at least quarterly.  
- Updates must be recorded with timestamps and author details.  
- Risk owners must sign off treatment plans.
